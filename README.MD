## 🏭 Project Overview
This portfolio project is designed to simulate the tasks of an **Operation Support Intern**. The dashboard presents an end-to-end analysis workflow of 2025 operational data for Harbour Energy Indonesia's assets (Natuna Sea Block A and Tuna Block). The goal is to provide actionable insights for executive management (COO, CFO, VP HSE) regarding operational efficiency.

## ⚙️ Analysis Framework

The analysis is broken down into 4 main operational pillars:

1.  ⛽ **Production Performance:** Tracking average production trends (\~13,800 boepd gross), individual well performance, and calculating production loss due to planned turnarounds.
2.  🦺 **Safety Performance:** Calculating the Total Recordable Incident Rate (TRIR) at 1.0 and breaking down incident types (dominated by Near Misses).
3.  📉 **Cost Efficiency:** Analyzing Unit OpEx ($/boe) per asset, where Block A achieved high efficiency ($12.2/boe), well below the global benchmark.
4.  💻 **Data Quality:** Documenting the validation and cleaning process for raw data anomalies (duplicates, negative values, future dates).

## 🎯 Key Findings & Recommendations

From the extraction of the 2025 operational data, three strategic initiatives are proposed with a **Total Projected Savings reaching \~$800K – 1.6M/year**:

  * 🔧 **Turnaround Duration Optimization:** Reducing the planned turnaround time from 15 days to 10 days through optimized parallel workflows.
  * 🚁 **Logistics Consolidation:** Sharing supply vessels and helicopter fleets for adjacent offshore assets in the Natuna Sea (Block A and Tuna).
  * 📡 **Predictive Maintenance Program:** Implementing condition-based monitoring (vibration sensors) to improve uptime metrics to \~97.5%.
